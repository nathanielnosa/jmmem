About Us

JMM Energies & Metals Ng Ltd was established since 2019, but officially registered with the corporate affairs commission of Nigeria (CAC) on 19th July 2023, with company registration number - RC 7061224 and SCUML certificate number(RN: SC 251510262). The company is a multi-service and diversified company, whose core expertise and business activities are highlighted below ;

  • • Consultant in Investment on Energy and Metals
  • • General Business Consultant in trades & investments
  • • HR Consultant (B2B), Supply of Engineering and Technical Field Labour and General Cleaning / Maintenance.
  • • Market Research Consultant (B2B, B2C)
  • • International Commission Agent, connecting buyers and sellers globally,Supply of Engineering Materials / Machineries.
The company is specialized in both manual and autotrading of different financial instruments such as energies (crude oil, natural gas ) and metals (gold, silver) online in the global financial market. The investment of our clients are fully insured by AXA Mansard Professional indemnity.
